Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, Profile Counter is completely free and open-source. You can use it for your personal or organization profiles without any limitations.

How does the counter work?

The counter works by tracking unique views of your badge. When someone loads your GitHub profile or repository that contains the badge, our server receives a request and increments your counter.

Can I see detailed analytics?

Yes, we provide a detailed analytics dashboard for registered users. You can see daily view counts, referrer information, and more.

Is my data private?

We respect your privacy. While we collect basic analytics data to make the counter work, we don't share any personal information with third parties. All analytics data is anonymized.

Can I contribute to this project?

Absolutely! The project is open-source and available on GitHub. We welcome contributions from the community, whether it's bug fixes, new features, or documentation improvements.
